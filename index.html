Supply the Classroom

Addressing the ongoing issue of underfunded classroom supplies in elementary education

The Problem

Many elementary classrooms lack consistent access to basic supplies such as tissues, disinfectant wipes, and hand sanitizer. These items are used daily but are often not included in sufficient school funding.

The Cause

School budgets prioritize larger academic resources, leaving everyday consumables overlooked and inconsistently restocked.

The Effects

When supplies run out, classrooms become harder to manage, less clean, and less effective for learning.

Why It Matters

Basic supplies directly support cleanliness, comfort, and a productive learning environment.
