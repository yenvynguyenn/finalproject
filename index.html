Classroom Supply Support Platform

A modern digital platform connecting teachers, parents, and communities to support essential classroom needs

The Problem

Elementary teachers often provide basic supplies like tissues, sanitizer, and wipes out of pocket because these items are overlooked or seen as optional. This creates financial strain and leaves classrooms under-resourced.

Why It Matters

Essential supplies are used daily and directly affect student health, comfort, and learning quality.

Without them, classrooms become harder to manage and less effective for students and teachers.

The Solution

A simple, centralized app/web platform where teachers can list needs and communities can donate directly in real time.

Key Features

  • Teacher classroom dashboards
  • Organized supply categories
  • Live donation tracking
  • Community request feed
  • Progress bars for supply goals
  • Urgent need notifications

Design Approach

The platform prioritizes simplicity and reduced cognitive load so users can quickly understand needs and take action without confusion.

Development Plan

  • Research: 2 weeks (user needs + existing systems)
  • Wireframes: 3 weeks (user flows + structure)
  • Design: 4 weeks (UI + prototype in Figma)
  • Testing: 3 weeks (feedback + improvements)

Impact

This platform reduces pressure on teachers, improves classroom environments, and makes community support for schools more accessible and organized.
