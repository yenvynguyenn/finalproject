Supply the Classroom

A simple, modern platform connecting teachers, parents, and communities to support essential classroom needs

The Problem

Elementary teachers often pay out of pocket for basic supplies like tissues, sanitizer, and wipes because these items are overlooked or seen as optional. This creates financial stress and leaves classrooms under-resourced.

Why It Matters

Essential supplies are used daily and directly impact student health, comfort, and learning quality.

Without them, classrooms become harder to manage and less effective for learning.

The Solution

A centralized app/web platform where teachers can list needs and communities can donate directly in real time.

Key Features

  • Teacher classroom dashboards
  • Organized supply categories
  • Live donation tracking
  • Community request feed
  • Progress bars for supply goals
  • Urgent need notifications

Design Approach

The platform is designed with simplicity and low cognitive load so users can quickly understand needs and take action easily.

Development Plan

  • Research: 2 weeks (user needs + existing systems)
  • Wireframes: 3 weeks (structure + flows)
  • Design: 4 weeks (UI + prototype)
  • Testing: 3 weeks (feedback + improvements)

Impact

This platform reduces pressure on teachers, improves classroom environments, and makes community support more organized and accessible.
